Job Description

We offer a very appealing internship to create Generative AI solutions and prototypes that support the overall studio experience. In our Digital Studio, we use Generative AI, Robotics, IoT, VR, and immersive experiences to show our capabilities to visitors. This internship is designed to offer an hands-on experience in the fast-evolving field of artificial intelligence: the opportunity to work on a specific – and visible - Generative AI-related project.

Responsibilities

  • Prompt engineering LLMs like GPTs, LLaMA, Claude for various NLP tasks for zero-shot/few-shot inference.
  • Undertake preprocessing of structured and unstructured data.
  • Develop & train ML/DL/NLP models according to requirements.
  • Extend ML libraries and frameworks to apply in NLP tasks.
  • Test and deploy trained models/systems on cloud services i.e. AWS/GCP/Azure.

Education + Experience

  • Persuing Bachelors Degree in Computer Science/ MSC in Computer Science.
  • Minimum of 3 months of experience in NLP, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning.
  • Knowledge of Deep Learning model optimization, transfer learning, and prompt engineering with LLMs like GPT3, GPT3.5, GPT4, and Claude.
  • Experience with state-of-the-art NLP models such as GPT, BERT, T5, and transformer-based models.
  • Familiarity with NLP techniques, libraries, and technologies, including Spacy, NLTK, Gensim, and Hugging Face transformers.
  • Knowledge on MLOps - FastAPI/Flask, Docker and/or Kubernetes, CI/CD, and Git is desirable.

Other Benifits

  • Internship Completion Certificates.
  • Project Completion letter.
  • Letter of Recommendations.
  • Get a Chance to work on Company live project.
  • Work on projects under the mentorship and guidance by developers.
